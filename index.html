NAVDISHA MEDICARE is a Multispecialty Clinic

We offer consultation and treatment for all medical conditions. NAVDISHA Medicare is founded by Dr. Navneet Arora, who is an Internal Medicine & Infectious Disease specialist. His current association is with Paras Hospital Panchkula, where he works as a consultant in the Department of Internal Medicine & Infectious Disease. He has been associated with prestigious institutes like PGI-Chandigarh, where he completed his Senior Residency & Government Medical College and Hospital (GMCH-32) Chandigarh, where he worked as an Assistant Professor. His Specialty training in Infectious Diseases is from the prestigious Christian Medical College (CMC) Vellore. Dr. Arora did his MBBS and MD in Internal Medicine from RGUHS Bangalore. He was the university topper and was awarded the best Outgoing Resident during his Post-Graduation.
